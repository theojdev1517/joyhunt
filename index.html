Simple on purpose.

Joy Hunt is not another feed, tracker, or productivity system. It is a quiet daily nudge to notice something good in the world you are already living in.

1. Get today’s hunt

Open the app and receive one universal prompt designed to work almost anywhere.

2. Go find it

The app’s job is to get out of the way while you notice your actual life.

3. Mark it found

Tap Found It. Add a photo or note only if you want to remember the moment.

You succeed because you noticed.

Built for quiet joy

No notification badges. No endless feed. No pressure to document everything. Joy Hunt celebrates the noticing itself.

Private by default.

Joy Hunt v0.1 stores your moments locally on your device. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no cloud upload. Sharing happens only when you explicitly choose it.

No account required

Start using Joy Hunt without signing in or handing over personal details.

Local-first storage

Your notes and photos stay on the device or browser where you created them.

Share only by choice

Moments are shared through your device’s native share sheet only when you tap Share.

Coming next

Joy Cloud, backup, sync, Joy Circles, and app-store releases are planned for later. The first version is intentionally small: one hunt, one moment, one day at a time.
